Integrated Behavioral Defense Plan

From Big Five Research to Actionable Social Engineering Strategy

Researcher: Daniel P Pedreira | Affiliation: Personal Portfolio

Executive Summary and Foundation

This plan converts personality trait analysis (Big Five) into a Level II Cybersecurity Defense Framework. It integrates Threat Intelligence and HR to create an adaptive training system, using **Behavioral Nudges** (subtle cues) that neutralize each employee's specific psychological vulnerability, moving security from a reactive to a proactive and predictive posture.

Implementation Stages

Behavioral Defense Framework (CRF - RCO)

1

Risk Assessment (CRF)

HR and Threat Intelligence collaborate to assign a **Behavioral Risk Factor (CRF)** to population clusters, identifying the exposure and most likely attack vector for each group.

Key Metric: CRF (Behavioral Risk Factor)

2

Intervention (NUDGES)

Implementation of gamified and personalized training using Nudges (behavioral cues) based on the dominant personality trait to build resilience (Ex: SOPA, TBYL).

Key Metric: TRAT (Training Attack Reaction Rate)

3

Operational Resilience (RCO)

Continuous measurement of simulation impact, adjusting the initial CRF and calculating the **Operational Behavioral Resilience (RCO)**, the final indicator of human risk.

Key Metric: RCO (Operational Behavioral Resilience)

Nudges and Behavioral Justification

SOPA (Agreeableness)

Secondary Confirmation Protocol (Out-of-Band).

**Exploits:** Trust Bias and Willingness to Help. **Intervention:** Forces a break from the primary communication (email/vishing) to verify the request through a secure channel (chat or phone). **Theory:** Inoculation (McGuire) – Creates resistance to emotional manipulation by establishing a rigid external rule.

STOP, BREATHE, VERIFY (Neuroticism)

Mandatory Pause under Stress/Urgency.

**Exploits:** Impulsivity and Anxiety under Urgency. **Intervention:** Trains the employee to pause the action and breathe (physical mechanism) before clicking links or responding to emotionally charged requests. **Theory:** Dual Process (Kahneman) – Forces the transition from System 1 (emotional) to System 2 (rational).

TBYL (Extraversion)

Think Before You Link/Share (Data Hygiene).

**Exploits:** Low Social Inhibition and *Oversharing*. **Intervention:** Makes the employee aware of how information published on social media is used in *spear-phishing* and *pretexting*. **Theory:** Privacy Calculus – Alters the perception of cost/benefit in data sharing.

SOURCE PRINCIPLE (Openness)

Channeling Curiosity.

**Exploits:** Novelty Seeking and Risk Acceptance. **Intervention:** Trains the employee to satisfy curiosity for innovation by seeking information exclusively through official channels (Intranet, IT), ignoring unsolicited external sources. **Theory:** Theory of Planned Behavior (TPB) – Redirects behavior to a secure channel.

RULE vs. RISK (Conscientiousness)

Formal Permission to Question.

**Exploits:** Blind Obedience to Authority. **Intervention:** Teaches that data security is the supreme rule. Explicitly grants permission for the employee to challenge an order if it appears to compromise security. **Theory:** Destructive Obedience (Milgram) – Restructures the hierarchy of priorities, placing security above speed.

Impact Measurement and Final Resilience (RCO)

The program effectiveness is measured through three interconnected metrics that culminate in the Operational Behavioral Resilience (RCO).

Process Metrics (Input)

  • **CRF (Behavioral Risk Factor):** Initial risk based on Big Five (Baseline Measurement).
  • **TRAT (Training Attack Reaction Rate):** Percentage of users who failed specific simulations (Failure Measurement).
  • **SOPA Compliance Rate:** % of users who confirmed the test request via an alternative channel (Nudge Measurement).

Final Metric (Output)

RCO (Operational Behavioral Resilience)

The resilience indicator that combines initial risk (CRF) with training effectiveness (TRAT).

RCO = 1 - ( (CRF * TRAT) + (1 - SOPA_Rate) )

Resilience Target: RCO > 0.85
